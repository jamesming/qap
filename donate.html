At present, our activities are limited to soliciting, capturing, and publishing audio histories. We'll also be providing information about how to take action that can aid our goals, like whom to write or call, etc.



For now, we are very-much in need of funds to grow The Queer Asylum Project. Your donations will be used to fund the critical next steps in our evolution, including costs of:



  • Incorporating as a 501(c)(3), so that donations will someday be tax-deductible
  • Local, state, and federal taxes
  • Web hosting
  • Audio journalists

Ultimately we, also hope to also establish a fund to help other asylum seekers with legal fees, relocation, and/or education.
